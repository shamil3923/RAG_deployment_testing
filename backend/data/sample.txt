Kaya is a fictional company used for this interview demo. Kaya builds an AI-powered
customer support platform. The platform ingests a company's help-center articles and
product documentation, then answers customer questions using retrieval-augmented
generation (RAG).

Founded in 2023, Kaya is headquartered in Colombo, Sri Lanka. The engineering team
works primarily with React on the frontend and Python (FastAPI) on the backend.

Kaya's RAG pipeline has three stages:
1. Ingestion - documents are split into overlapping chunks and embedded into vectors.
2. Retrieval - an incoming question is embedded and the most similar chunks are fetched
   from a vector database (Chroma in this demo).
3. Generation - the retrieved chunks are passed as context to a large language model
   (Qwen, served through NVIDIA's OpenAI-compatible API) which writes the final answer.

Kaya offers three pricing tiers: Starter at 0 dollars per month for up to 100 questions,
Pro at 49 dollars per month for up to 10,000 questions, and Enterprise with custom pricing
and on-premise deployment. The free Starter tier does not include analytics.
